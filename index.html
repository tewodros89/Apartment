About Studio

Apartment ABC features brand new studio, 1, and 2 bedroom apartments in Quincy, MA. Apartment homes come equipped with gourmet kitchens, modern appliances, spacious closets, and washer/dryer. Community amenities include state of the art fitness center, heated pool, dog park, rooftop deck, and outdoor kitchen with grilling stations and fireplace.
